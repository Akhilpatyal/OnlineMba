Who Can Apply?

  • Bachelor’s Degree or Level 6 Equivalent in any discipline
  • IELTS Score of 5.5
  • No IELTS? No worries! Take our free internal English assessment and qualify easily

Supporting Documents Checklist

Applicants must submit the following documents along with their application:

  • Academic Documents:

    • Official transcripts of Bachelor's / Level 6 qualifications
    • Certificate of completion of Level 6 diploma or Bachelor's degree

  • Professional Documents:

    • Updated CV detailing work experience in relevant fields
    • Letter of Experience (if applicable)

  • English Proficiency Evidence:

    • IELTS certificate (if applicable) or equivalent
    • results from LAAT’s mandatory internal English assessment (if opting for this route)

  • Identification:

    • A copy of a valid passport and national ID (for proof of residence)
    • A passport-size photo

  • Personal Statement:

    • A 500-word statement outlining your career goals and how this program aligns with them

  • Letter of Recommendation

    • A letter from a professional or academic referee supporting your application

Delivery Mode Information

Hybrid Learning Explained:

  • Self-Paced Learning (60%)

    Engage with interactive digital resources, recorded lectures, and self-directed activities. Designed for flexibility, this mode allows learners to progress at their own pace while balancing other commitments.

  • Live Sessions (40%)

    Participate in scheduled virtual classes and workshops led by academic experts. These sessions are interactive, with opportunities for Q&A, group discussions, and tailored feedback on your projects.
